Comprehensive Range of Electrical Control Panels

Ess Bee Trade Box is a leading manufacturer and supplier of precision-engineered electrical control panels. Designed for superior performance, durability, and safety, our products cater to a wide array of industrial and commercial applications.

Power Control Center (PCC) Panel

Our PCC Panels are modular structured electrical control panels used to control power supply in large industrial and commercial units. They efficiently manage power supplied to heavy machinery, equipment, motors, and transformers. Features include single/multiple incomers, bus couplers with interlocking, protective relays, and options for top/middle/horizontal bus bar chambers, and copper/aluminum bus bars.

High Tension (HT) Panel

Our High Tension (HT) Panels are designed for controlling, protecting, and isolating electrical systems operating at high voltages. Built with superior components and safety mechanisms, they ensure reliable power distribution and protection for transformers, motors, and main power lines in large-scale industrial applications.

Low Tension (LT) Panel

Designed for low voltage applications, our LT Panels are used for controlling and distributing power efficiently within commercial buildings and industrial settings. They are built for safety, reliability, and ease of maintenance, serving as a crucial link in the power distribution chain.

Automatic Mains Failure (AMF) Panel

AMF Panels automatically switch power supply to a standby generator upon mains failure and switch back when mains power is restored. This ensures uninterrupted power supply for critical operations, minimizing downtime and protecting sensitive equipment.
