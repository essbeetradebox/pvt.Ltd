Metal Fabrication

Crafting the Foundation of Every Electrical Panel

Material Selection Cutting Punching & Drilling Bending & Forming Welding & Assembly Grinding & Finishing

Precision in Metalwork

At Ess Bee Trade Box, metal fabrication is where our electrical panel designs take physical form. We utilize state-of-the-art machinery and skilled craftsmanship to transform raw metal sheets into robust, precise enclosures, ensuring every panel provides the ultimate protection and structural integrity for its critical components.

Material Selection

The first step in crafting a durable electrical panel is selecting the right material. We choose from high-grade mild steel, galvanized steel, or stainless steel, depending on the panel's intended environment and required corrosion resistance. This foundational choice ensures the longevity and performance of the enclosure.

Cutting

Precision cutting transforms raw metal sheets into the exact shapes and sizes required for each panel component. Utilizing advanced laser cutters and shears, we achieve highly accurate cuts with clean edges, minimizing waste and ensuring perfect fitment in subsequent assembly stages.

Punching & Drilling

With CNC punching and drilling machines, we create precise holes and cutouts for component mounting, ventilation, and cable entry. This automated process ensures every opening is perfectly positioned, guaranteeing seamless integration of electrical devices and optimal airflow within the panel.

Bending & Forming

Using advanced press brakes, flat metal sheets are precisely bent and formed into the three-dimensional structures of the panel enclosure. This critical step creates the rigid framework and accurate dimensions necessary for a secure and well-aligned electrical panel.

Welding & Assembly

Our skilled welders meticulously join the fabricated metal components to create the complete panel structure. Strong, clean welds are essential for the panel's durability, structural integrity, and to maintain its environmental protection. Internal mounting plates and supports are also securely integrated.

Grinding & Finishing

After welding, all rough edges and weld seams are meticulously ground and smoothed. This crucial finishing step ensures a flawless surface for subsequent powder coating, enhances the panel's aesthetics, and eliminates any sharp edges for improved safety during handling and installation.
