Our Comprehensive Solutions

Delivering precision-engineered electrical panels through meticulous design, expert fabrication, and robust integration.

Our Electrical Panel Manufacturing Process

At Ess Bee Trade Box Pvt Ltd, we follow a streamlined and precise process to ensure every electrical panel meets the highest standards of quality and performance.

Design & Engineering

We begin with in-depth analysis of your requirements, creating detailed electrical schematics and panel layouts using advanced CAD software for optimal functionality and efficiency.

Learn More

Metal Fabrication

Our skilled technicians precisely cut, bend, and weld metal sheets to form robust enclosures and internal mounting structures, ensuring durability and compliance with design specifications.

Learn More

Powder Coating

We apply high-quality powder coatings to the fabricated enclosures, providing superior corrosion resistance, excellent insulation, and a durable, aesthetically pleasing finish.

Learn More

Component Integration

Experienced technicians meticulously mount electrical components, wire circuits according to schematics, and ensure secure connections for flawless electrical operation.

Learn More

Quality Assurance & Testing

Every panel undergoes rigorous multi-point quality checks and functional testing, including dielectric strength tests and IP certification validation, to guarantee safety and performance.

Learn More

Final Assembly & Dispatch

After passing all quality checks, panels are securely assembled, packaged for safe transport, and dispatched with all necessary documentation and certifications.
