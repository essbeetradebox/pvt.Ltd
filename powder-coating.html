Powder Coating

Durable Protection, Flawless Finish

Cleaning & Pre-treatment Powder Application Curing & Baking Quality Check

The Ultimate Protective Layer

At Ess Bee Trade Box, our powder coating process is more than just aesthetics; it's about providing superior durability and protection for your electrical panel enclosures. This advanced finishing technique ensures resistance against corrosion, chemicals, and wear, extending the lifespan and maintaining the pristine appearance of our products.

Cleaning & Pre-treatment

A flawless finish begins with a perfectly prepared surface. Our multi-stage cleaning and pre-treatment process rigorously removes all oils, grease, and contaminants from the metal. This ensures optimal adhesion of the powder coat and enhances the panel's resistance to corrosion, forming a robust foundation for the protective layer.

Powder Application

Using advanced electrostatic spray guns, finely ground powder particles are precisely applied to the pre-treated metal surface. The electrostatic charge ensures uniform coverage, even in complex geometries, resulting in a consistent and smooth protective layer that is free from drips or runs.

Curing & Baking

After powder application, the coated panels enter a high-temperature curing oven. Here, the powder melts and chemically cross-links, transforming into a smooth, hard, and extremely durable finish. This baking process ensures maximum adhesion and creates a resilient protective layer that withstands harsh conditions.

Quality Check

Every powder-coated panel undergoes rigorous quality checks. We inspect for uniform thickness, adhesion, color consistency, and overall finish integrity. This meticulous examination ensures that each enclosure meets our stringent quality standards, providing long-lasting protection and a premium appearance.
